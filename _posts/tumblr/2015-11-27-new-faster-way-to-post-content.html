Your very own Website Winter Warmer from Givey is now live! More rewarding than any spiced gingerbread latte, all digital content is now opened us to donations and social Giving! With one click, your empathy and fundraising is turned into action as you can now upload via the platforms you already feel comfortable with by clicking their icon in the donation flow.


If you haven’t yet, all you need to do is link your social accounts in your Givey settings which you will be directed to when you hit one of the icons and voila, you can donate to a cause inspired by your own content, and that can inspire others!

Why should you be excited about this we hear you ask?

Uploading content to Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and YouTube can become habitual, but what about Giving? Here at Givey, we want to make donating a habit too, and with these new icons all the channels you already love and upload to can make the difference in the communities that Givey charities and projects impact.

If you are a charity or project administrator, this process allows you to post content quickly to your Givey page with a provider you are already comfortable with, illustrating the journey of your cause from image, video, article or many more to investment in a solution. For Business, see what your users are up to as the icons allows investment in the marketing of your social responsibilty too, as they can contribute to telling important stories.


Think there are any more icons we should include? Pop the URL to the box below the icons and drop us a message at support@givey.com.

Givey this Christmas to contribute to someone else’s merriment.
