An early Christmas present has arrived from our lovely tech team! You can now copy and paste some simple code into your web pages and blogs to get your very own Givey donation button.

Whether you are from a charity or you’re raising funds, make it super easy for your community to support you. The button will take supporters directly into the ‘giving’ flow, with your cause preselected for them. 

To get hold of your button, once you’ve set up your page on Givey click into your ‘Settings’. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and you’ll see the set of codes you need to copy into your webpage to render your very own button!
