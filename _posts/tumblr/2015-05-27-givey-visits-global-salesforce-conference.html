On Thursday 21st May, some of our team had the opportunity to make our way to what Keynote speaker Keith Block described as the ‘Largest software conference in the history of the UK.’

Salesforce, global cloud computing giants born and thriving since 1999, described how with other companies roughly 77% of customers are not engaged with their organisations, but explored how customer success ecosystems and services can bump these numbers.

The next step in relation to Givey is similarly discovering what devices and mediums help people to engage more with our platform to promote innovative content that is self-incentive and engages with the specific content users are drawn to.

Following a customer driven roadmap, we aim to learn and develop what the market of giving requires to become a community which invites donors through authentic connections to join and support important causes to them through inspiring material.

by Emma@Givey
