We wanted to share some fantastic news with you all today - we’ve been officially listed as one of YouTube’s accredited crowdfunding sites!!

“Crowdfunding links can be used to drive awareness and support for your creative campaigns” - Google

This is really brilliant news for us at Givey, as the listing helps to deepen the ability to connect with and share content to make real change.

It’s also really great news for all of our supporters, charities and projects too as this opens the doors for collaborations with YouTubers and content creators. It’s the start of more meaningful relationships with influencers who want to help do good and fund causes that matter. We’re connecting, collaborating and helping to bring good into the world!

So thank you, YouTube, and here’s to inspiring real change through content!
