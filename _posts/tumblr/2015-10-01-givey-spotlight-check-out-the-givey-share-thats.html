Climb | September

​Raising money for charity Climb, the National Information and Advice Centre for Metabolic Diseases have created our most popular Share, raising over £17,570 so far! Boosted with mountains of support, here are a couple of our favourite messages. Don’t forget you can make a difference too - hit the link to support.

Launch | October

​Launch Young Adults is a mentorship organisation for unlocking potential in young adults. With your support, Lia and her team can help ambitious teens and young adults bridge the gap between what they don’t learn in school, but need to know for the real world.

“Through digital content, public speaking, and one-on-one mentorship we can help those with goals realise their dreams.”

​Support Launch Young People​

Refugee Appeal | In the News​

​With over 3 million people leaving to seek refuge from the crisis in Syria, Givey set up a specific campaign to help channel your donations to the official charities helping in the relief effort. Take a look below to see who they are and give what you can.
