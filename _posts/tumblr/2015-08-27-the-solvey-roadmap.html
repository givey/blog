If you followed the Solvey project, you will be aware that our founder Dave Erasmus and Louis Cole were on the hunt for innovative and inspiring ideas to help others. The project they chose out of many contenders is One Wave, who were our first live project on Givey! 

To see the #Solvey roadmap, take a look at the below infographic:

Are you a compassionate problem solver?

You don’t have to be from the UK or even a registered charity, you just need to have the initiative to create solutions for those in need. We support individuals, groups or organisations that meet our criteria and wish to help others using our unique content donation mechanism, the Givey Share, to raise the funds they need to alleviate some of the world’s most prominent crises and concerns.
