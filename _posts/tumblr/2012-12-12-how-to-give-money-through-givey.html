Giving money with Givey is easy. 

Online:

You can give directly to a charity by hitting the ‘Make Donation’ button on the charity’s profile page and entering the amount you wish to give.

You can also give to charities that your friends support, by hitting the 'Make Donation’ button on their personal profile. Money will go to their supported charity.

Twitter:

And for the more socially minded, you can give to a charity – directly or through your friends – by twitter: 

Tweet #GIVEY #[CHARITY OR USER GIVEYTAG] AMOUNT IN POUNDS, e.g. ‘GIVEY #AMNESTYINT 2’ or ‘#GIVEY #DAVE 2’

Where does the money go?

For every £1 that you donate, 100% of this goes direct to the charity!  We work with PayPal Giving Fund who process every donation through PayPal, claim the Gift-Aid for each donation, and send the payment to each charity.
