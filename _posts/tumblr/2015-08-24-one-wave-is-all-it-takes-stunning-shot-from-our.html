One Wave is all it Takes

Stunning shot from our featured Global Project, OneWave.

Check them out here and help them open another beach Surf Experience to get people talking about mental health and wellbeing. $5,000 will enable hundreds of sufferers to learn to catch a wave, hang out with people experiencing similar difficulties and get some endorphine-inducing exercise.

Anything you can give will massively help Grant and Joel, the two inspiring guys who won the Solvey project to launch their idea and help others all over the world.

We’ll keep you posted about the guys and how they’re spending your generously donated funds and the people you’re investing in to help. 

It’s a truly incredible thing to be a part of and your contribution will really change lives. From us all, thank you.
